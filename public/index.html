Introduction to Campaign: Grimlark Darkness Within

The world is very dark and grim, you get this feeling that this world is dusk and descerted. You see off in the distance darkness, the towns and buildings ran down and some even destroyed. The only light emitted are from townfolk lanterns & street lights that are powered by some sort of magic or fuel you can't really tell...

The emotions on the people's faces look saddened and hopeless, living their lives in labour as their only form of survival. Even if you were thinking of finding out more information, that knowledge is very scarced, and frown upon in this society, almost like an enforced conduct has washed over the minds.

You all being in a tavern that has a strange aroma, almost a must frangrance that smells of false riches. There is however a desirable bulletin board with bold letters inscribed as "Jobs" with a gold trim around the edges. You get a sense somebody cares alot about this board. All of you have no relations with eachother while you all sit at different tables and chairs in this grimy place. But you all are drawn to this bulletin board, specifically a certain task, one that your eye's lock on.. "Slay Lucifer 1000 gold".

Infront of the board a woman with a cloak and daggers equipped to her legs, stands determined, staring at the board.

Woman with Cloak & Daggers -> Skylar Silvermask

  • Split Personality

    • Adventure driven, hardwork, enjoys a challenge, caring/team oriented

    Seeks to train the adventurers before fighting Lucifer

  • Backstory:

    • Came through back alley brawls, trains all her life in the dumps and alleys

    She needs this money to feed and take care of the orphans and odd ball creatures that live in Grimlark

  • Second Personality

    • Dark, shady, evil personality that is controlled by the council that runs the city Her second personality has no correlation, and her memories are seperate. (She basically lures adverturers to their ends and that's how the council controls Grimlark by killing and pilfering all adventurers of their loot and belongings.)

    She turns against the party in the last stage where she leads the group to an alley that has been remoured as the "Lucifer's Alley/Corner" which is actually an ambush by the council.

    The police force (controlled by council) find the party & Skylar training and inform the party that Lucifer's Alley is causing mischief in the city and that they need help from the party and Skylar. (A ploy/regular routine set by the council)

Lucifer's Alley/Corner

The player's get ambushed in the alley 2 archers (1 on either side of the broken down buildings) These archers have special arrowheads that are dipped in a chemical that incapacitates the players after 3 failed CON saves.

2 Archers on the top of the building infront

3 Body guards that are enclosing the players in from the back

Dialogue: A dark figure in a trench coat snaps his fingers and Skylar's personality changes, in seconds, the emotion on her face wethers and all there is left is this devilish grin. In a flash the trench coat figure vanishes into a black sparrow type bird and flies away. The direction is unknown due to the darkness and the buildings blocking the angle in seconds.

Save Skylar:

Incapacitate her, the archer's firing the dipped arrows if getting a hold of one and striking Skylar with it will make her incapacitated instantly as the chemical is super effective against her.

Alternatively, her split personality lasts 5 minutes = 50 turns in combat

Thirdly, reducing her hitpoints to 0 works as well.

Info: Buildings on the sides are broken down, archers are shooting from 1st floor (10ft). Archers from infront are shooting from the top of the building (40ft).

If Skylar is saved, the player's continue to get lodging and food, etc. As Skylar, is a relative of the person that owns the tavern.
